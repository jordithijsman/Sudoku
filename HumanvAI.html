\ Human versus AI


On this page you have the opportunity to test your skills against the different algorithms. Start by picking a difficulty, the system will generate a random sudoku for you. Don't forget to enter your name to be featured on the highscores!

The algorithms you play against here have their speed limited to give you a fighting chance. But their actual time is recorded on the highscores.

If you need some help getting started with a sudoku then click here.



Press "play" to start the clock! When you think you have the solution your can press "done". The system will then pause the clock and check your answer.
