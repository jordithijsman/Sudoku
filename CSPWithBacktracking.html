\ Sudoku Algorithms

The CSP with backtracking algorithm is a combination of the two. First the CSP algorithm will be executed, If CSP can't solve the sudoku on its own, it will try its best at solving it and the backtracking algorithm will finish solving the sudoku using the values it got from CSP.

CSP with backtracking is the fastest algorithm this site uses to solve sudokus.
In reality CSP will never need backtracking to solve a 2x2 sudoku, but to give a simple example we will use a 2x2 sudoku to explain the working of the algorithm.
To help you understand the algorithm the sudoku to the right will show the different steps but you have to follow the steps and go back and forwards like the steps tell you. To start press the "Show steps" button.
Whenever you want to go to the end and see all the steps you can press the "Show all steps" button. You will then also see the solved sudoku.
