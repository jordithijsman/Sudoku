Rules of a sudoku


On this site you will be able to play sudokus from different difficulty levels. A sudoku is a square consisting of squares whose dimension is defined by the difficulty level. The easy sudokus consist of 2x2 squares, normal sudokus of 3x3 squares and hard sudokus of 4x4 squares.

For each level the upper limit of values is different: for easy sudokus it's 4, for normal sudokus 9 and for hard sudokus 16. To solve a sudoku each row, column and square have to contain all numbers from 1 to the upper limit. It's not allowed to have a number twice in a row, column or square.
