Welcome to this website on Sudoku algorithms.


This website will explain the different ways in which humans and computers solve Sudokus. Both use algorithms to find a solution but some are more efficient than others. This platform will let you compete against different computer algorithms and teach you why you might be faster than some but stand no chance against others.

If you already know how a Sudoku works you can click here to play against the AI. If you need some help getting started, click here to find out how to solve a Sudoku (the human way). Or you can just check out the algorithms the computer uses by clicking here.

You can also switch between these activities using the navigation bar at the top of the page.
